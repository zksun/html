Calculation of estimated node income

  • The above table has provided daily rewards, which can be multiplied by the corresponding number of days to calculate monthly and annual income

How do nodes improve income?

  • The network scale is calculated based on 20 proposal nodes and 80 verification nodes.

  • Therefore, the total stake amount of proposal nodes is 20 * 2000 = 40000, and the total stake amount of verification nodes is 100 * 400 = 40000

  • Then, for the proposal node, increase the stake amount of n units of PRG, the income will increase n/(40000+n) on the previous basis

  • For the verification node, increase the stake amount of m units of PRG, the income will increase m/(40000+m) on the previous basis
